On this website you will find information about the environmental consequences of computer devices and the steps to limit your impact.

Brief Introduction

Computer devices are one of the biggest contributors to greenhouse gas emissions and e-waste as it contains multiple toxic chemicals. In just 2019 alone we have generated over 53 million tons of e-waste that is harmful to the environment and human health. Computer devices' very existence is a form of greenhouse gas emission as the production and disposal of these devices require a lot of energy and valuable materials. The usage of computers is also damaging to the environment as you have to maintain, manage, and upgrade networks and servers.
