On this website you will find information about the environmental consequences of computer devices and the steps to limit your impact.

Breif Introduction

Computer devices are one of the biggest contributors to greenhouse gas emissions. Their very existence is a form of greenhouse gas emission as the production and disposal of these devices requires a lot of energy and valuable materials. The usage of computers is also damaging to the environment as you have to maintain, manage, and upgrade networks and servers.
