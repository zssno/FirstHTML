Computer Device Production

The manufacturing, shipping, usage, and disposal of computers consume a lot of energy that is usually generated from the burning of fossil fuels. This greatly contributes to the global warming crisis as huge amounts of greenhouse gasses are released. Burning of fossil fuels is the largest contributor to greenhouse gas emissions and the production of computers directly play a hand in it. Inside these devices are precious materials and are often found through unjust labor. The refinement of these materials is also often extremely expensive and energy intensive. They are also usually just disposed of in landfills when they can easily be recycled and reused.
